About Ryan's Guitar Shop

Ryan's Guitar Shop, a true gem in the heart of eastern Canada, has been a mecca for musicians since its doors first opened in 1989. Founded by Ryan MacLeod, a passionate luthier with an ear for exceptional tone, the shop quickly became renowned for its unparalleled selection of instruments and expert craftsmanship.

A Legacy of Excellence

Over the past three and a half decades, Ryan's Guitar Shop has solidified its reputation as the premier destination for guitarists of all levels. Its walls are adorned with an impressive array of instruments, from vintage classics to cutting-edge models, catering to every style and preference.

Celebrity Clientele

The shop's reputation has attracted numerous famous musicians over the years. Legendary artists like Bryan Adams, Alanis Morissette, and members of Rush have all been spotted perusing the aisles, often leaving with prized instruments that would later grace stages around the world.

Rare and Unique Offerings

What truly sets Ryan's Guitar Shop apart is its remarkable collection of rare and vintage guitars. The shop's climate-controlled vault houses some of the most sought-after instruments in existence, including: A 1959 Gibson Les Paul Standard, often referred to as the "Holy Grail" of electric guitars A pre-war Martin D-45, known for its unparalleled tone and historical significance A 1950s Fender Broadcaster, one of the earliest Telecaster models ever produced These treasures, along with many others, make Ryan's Guitar Shop a pilgrimage site for guitar enthusiasts from across the globe.
